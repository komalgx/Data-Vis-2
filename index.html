EXPLORING AUSTRALIA'S FORESTS

This collection of visualizations explores the distribution and evolution of plantation areas across Australia, focusing on the major plantation types: hardwood and softwood. The charts illustrate how plantation areas vary across different states and time periods, providing insights into regional forestry practices and trends. With dynamic options to filter by year, these visualizations help to highlight significant changes in plantation sizes over the 2011-2014 period. This data sheds light on the sustainable management and growth of forest plantations, which play a crucial role in the country's forestry industry and environmental conservation efforts.
